# Digital Dumbing SOPs
## Current SOPs:
1. Italicized are apps we must refrain from generally, and will be session limited using a blocking app like StayFree. Either use these on a laptop or limit these.
2. Internet should be off generally on your phone, and there should be a once in a day time to get phone online, the entertainment time (Like an Eritrean Internet Cafe) Have online entertainment time right next to messages answering time, and online entertainment should be shared
3. Ask for Internet Searches to People
4. Check for Messages only for during entertainment times day and tell everyone that you will call them
5. Ask for directions 
6. Have someone order an Uber for you
7. Laptop does not leave a dedicated place
8. Use Physical Cash instead of Digital 
9. Keep your phone for 2FA in office 
10. Have office timings and entertainment timings and don't access laptop outside of those
11. Have online times during office hours when you can access the browser and the internet 
12. Write down tasks from Calendar + Todoist to Paper during planning time, planning time is offline 
13. Apps only sync once a day during dedicated online time
14. Make a list of your personal online tasks and batch them
15. During work phone should be away
16. You can also switch to online for making WhatsApp calls if the number is offline
17. Phone will not remain in easy access with you it will be in a bag
18. Office can go mobile when traveling 
19. Stay in the moment
20. Device free entertainment is always better
21. Keep BnW

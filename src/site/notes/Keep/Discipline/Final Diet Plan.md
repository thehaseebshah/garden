#### **Morning Protocol (Metabolism Kickstart)**
- **07:00 AM:** **Chia Seeds** (1 tbsp soaked in 1 glass water overnight).
- **08:00 AM:** **Green Tea** (Plain or with Mint. No sugar).
- **08:30 AM:** **Activity:** 30 Minute Brisk Walk.
#### **12:00 PM: The "Super Brunch" (Main Fueling Meal)**
- **Carbs:** 3 Tablespoons (raw measure) **Jao/Jae (Barley or Oat) Dalia**.
- **Protein/Fat:** 4 **Whole Eggs** (Boiled, poached, or dry-fried. No oil).
- **Liquid:** 1 Cup **Elaichi Milk** (Full-fat).
- **Nuts:** Max 7 Almonds OR 2 Walnuts.
#### **04:00 PM: Pre-Asr / Pre-Workout Snack (The Energy Spike)**
- **The Fuel (Dates):** **2 Dates**.
    - _Role:_ These provide immediate glucose to your muscles so you don't feel dizzy on the field.
- **The Hydration (Lemo Pani):** 1 glass of cold water + Half Lemon squeezed + **1 pinch of regular white salt**.
    - _Role:_ The salt and water prevent cramping; the lemon cuts acidity.
- **Optional Volume:** If you are very hungry, you may still have **2 seasonal fruits** (Apple/Guava) here. If the dates satisfy you, skip the fruit.

#### **05:00 PM: Evening Activity**
- **Activity:** Football (Light intensity/Technical skills).
#### **07:00 PM: Dinner (Recovery)**
- **Carbs:** **½ Roti** (Multigrain preferred) OR 8 Teaspoons Rice.
    - _Note:_ Since you are playing sport, do not skip this Roti. It helps you sleep.
- **Protein:**
    - A bowl of home-cooked Salan (Meat/Veg). _Use the ice cube trick to remove floating oil._
- **Mandatory Sides:**
    - **Salad:** Large portion (Cucumber, Lettuce, Tomato).
    - **Raita:** 1 small bowl (Zeera Raita).
    - **Chicken Strips** (100g) sautéed in 1 tsp oil/ghee.
#### **09:00 PM: Bedtime Protocol**
- **Supplement:** **Ispaghol** (Psyllium Husk) in water.

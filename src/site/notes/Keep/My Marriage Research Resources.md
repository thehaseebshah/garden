## The Criterias I had:
1. Fulfills all religious obligations, and preferably also fulfills voluntary acts of worship
2. Sharih Niqab and Pardah
3. At least a short Islamic course from an Islamic organization
4. Wants to live a simple, non-luxurious life
5. Passionate for Dawah
6. Affiliated with an Islamic organization
7. Regularly listens to (or at least has listened to) popular Islamic Scholars and/or speakers
8. Prefers not to do a Full-time job after marriage
9. Is from respectable family
10. Homemaking qualities
11. Father is Alim, or at least family is Islamic and conservative

## Things to Check for in Marriage Discussions
- Will she present herself before parents on the family meeting day

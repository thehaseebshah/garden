# Digital Dumbing SOPs
## Current SOPs:
### 🏗️ Environmental & Device Boundaries
- **Fixed Stations:** Laptop does not leave its dedicated place; keep it at the office or a specific home desk. (paused)
- **The "Out of Sight" Rule:** Phone stays in a bag, not a pocket; it remains away during work. 
- **BnW:** Keep the screen in Black & White (BnW)
- Prefer audio/text over images/videos. 
- **Offline First:** Internet is off by default. Treat the phone like an "Eritrean Internet Cafe" with strictly scheduled online windows. 
- **Analog Tools:** Use physical cash; use paper notepads for notes; take directions from people, not GPS.
- No LLMs

### 💬 Communication & Social SOPs
- **Synchronous over Asynchronous:** Call instead of texting; tell people you will call them back rather than chatting all day.
- **The "Letter" Mindset:** Treat non-urgent texts like physical mail—to be answered in batches, not immediately.
- **Scheduled Connectivity:** Check messages only during designated "entertainment/answering" times.
- **Outsourcing Tech:** Ask people for internet searches or have someone else order your Uber to avoid opening the app yourself.

### 📈 Workflow & Productivity
- **Analog Planning:** Planning time is offline. Transfer Calendar and Task items to paper for the day's use.
- **Batching:** Sync apps only once a day; list and batch personal online tasks to avoid "quick checks" turning into rabbit holes.
- **Timed Access:** Define "Office Timings" vs. "Entertainment Timings." Only access the browser during specific windows. (paused)

### 🧘 Mindset & Maintenance
- **Presence:** Stay in the moment; remember that device-free entertainment is always superior.
- **The Detox Rule:** Don't use the detox timer when you don't have to.
- **Travel Flexibility:** Office work can go mobile when traveling, but maintain the boundaries as much as possible.
- Don't use western LLMs for personal chats

# Digital Dumbing SOPs
## Current SOPs:

- **Fixed Stations:** Laptop does not leave its dedicated place; keep it at the office or a specific home desk.
- **The "Out of Sight" Rule:** Phone stays in a bag, not a pocket; it remains away during work.
- **BnW:** Keep the screen in Black & White (BnW)
- Prefer audio/text over images/videos.
- **Offline First:** Internet is off by default. Treat the phone like an "Eritrean Internet Cafe" with strictly scheduled online windows.
- **Analog Tools:** Use physical cash; use paper notepads for notes; take directions from people, not GPS.
- No LLMs

- **Timed Access:** Define "Office Timings" vs. "Entertainment Timings." Only access the browser during specific windows.

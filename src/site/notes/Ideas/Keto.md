## Peanut Butter and Milk
**Peanut Milk Recipe** (2:10-3:04)

1. Take 250g (one-quarter kilogram) of local, shelled peanuts. You can make milk with the skins on, but removing them will give a better color.
2. Soak the peanuts in water for 5-6 hours.
3. Clean and wash the soaked peanuts.
4. Place the peanuts in a grinder. Add 2 to 3 glasses of water, depending on your desired thickness.
5. Grind the mixture to make milk.
6. Strain the milk. You can then add more water to the strained peanut pulp and grind again to get a total of 4 glasses of peanut milk from 250g of peanuts.

**Peanut Butter Recipe** (4:39-5:45)

1. Take 250g of peanuts and wash them thoroughly.
2. Lightly roast the peanuts for 2-3 minutes on low heat.
3. Place the roasted peanuts in a cloth and rub them to remove the skins. Discard the skins.
4. Wash and soak the peanuts again.
5. Put the peanuts in a grinder. You can add honey for children.
6. Grind the peanuts for 3-4 minutes. Scrape down the sides of the grinder as needed. Do not add water or any other liquid.
7. Continue grinding until the peanut butter is ready.

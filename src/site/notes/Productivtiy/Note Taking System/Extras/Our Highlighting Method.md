Highlight important passages from a book/blog etc.
For books 
- The preferred way is physical books. You can write on the margins if you want to add additional info, or use sticky notes.
- When reading, place a slash in the corner of any page containing relevant information. Underline crucial sentences or names that stand out. Avoid excessive commentary—trust your brain to recall why a section was marked
- If a passage is particularly valuable, place a star next to it. Additionally, put a star in the corner of the page for easy identification.
- For structured arguments (e.g., “Three reasons why X”), number each point.
- For Digital Books on a tablet: do digital annotations in a similar way

For blogs
- Highlight and save to Obsidian. But don't bookmark too often.

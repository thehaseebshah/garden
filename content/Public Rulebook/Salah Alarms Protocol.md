# The following is the protocol for [[Salah]] Alarms
We set up alarms 45 minutes before Jamah time.

We snooze for 15 minutes when it rings, and make up our mind for [[Salah]]. 

After the next ring we snooze for 10 mins and pack up and leave after 10 minutes, reach 15 minutes before Jamah.
